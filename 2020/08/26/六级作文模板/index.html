whether to major in science or humanities at college

“Science changes the world.”, a simple but enlightening remark on science by a celebrity. The profound meaning lying in this saying persuades us that provided that our human society intends to acquire and enjoy long-time prosperity. It is advisable for humans to maintain their concept, to major in science.

“科学改变世界”，这是一位名人关于科学的一句简单却富有启发性的评论。这句话的深刻含义告诉我们，如果我们的人类社会打算获得和享受长期繁荣。人类最好保持自己的观念，以科学为专业。

Firstly, science, without a doubt, is the stepping stone which enriches learners’ minds, enhances their learning efficiency, ensures more fruitful results, and enables them to go further on the way to education.

首先，毫无疑问，科学是踏脚石，它丰富了学习者的心灵，提高了他们的学习效率，确保更丰硕的成果，使他们在教育的道路上走得更远。

Meanwhile, one’s career can be compared to building his or her own skyscraper. If “humanities” constructs the base of the skyscraper, “science” would makes the building taller and firmer.

与此同时，一个人的职业生涯可以比作建造自己的摩天大楼。如果说“人文”是摩天大楼的基础，那么“科学”将使摩天大楼更高更坚固。

Indeed, no one could survive and prosper without science. To be specific, without science, a new employee is prone to confine himself to low efficiency, poor abilities, and narrow-mindedness.

的确，没有科学，没有人能够生存和繁荣。具体地说，如果没有科学知识，新员工很容易把自己局限在低效率、低能力和狭隘的环境中。

In conclusion, science lays a solid foundation for students’ academic success, as well as for them to be qualified competitors in the future. Only science can the young gain life-long benefits from education and work.

总之，科学为学生的学业成功奠定了坚实的基础，也为他们将来成为合格的竞争者。只有科学才能使年轻人从教育和工作中获得终生的益处。
